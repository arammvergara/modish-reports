Highlights & Recommendations

Wins This Week

  • Revenue up +34.7% WoW — total social revenue jumped from £6,689 to £9,010.50, the strongest revenue week observed in this data window.
  • Paid Social revenue surge (+35.8%) — Facebook/Meta campaigns are converting at higher order values, suggesting either improved creative or a higher-intent audience in the cohort.
  • Organic Social AOV improving — organic revenue rose +27.4% despite fewer sessions, pointing to better quality traffic from current content.
  • Longer organic dwell time (+23.4%) — organic visitors are spending significantly more time on site (101s vs 82s), a positive signal for content engagement quality.
  • New user growth (+3.8% paid, stable organic) — paid campaigns are efficiently reaching new audiences week on week.

Areas to Watch

  • Organic Social traffic declining (−10.3%) — sessions and conversions from organic channels are both falling. Content output or posting frequency may need review.
  • Instagram nearly invisible — only 16 organic sessions this period, down −30.4%. The Instagram organic channel is severely under-driving traffic relative to its potential.
  • TikTok negligible (2 sessions) — essentially no organic TikTok traffic. This remains the brand's biggest untapped organic channel given the target audience behaviour on HomeToK.
  • Paid Social bounce rate spike (+118.5%) — bounce rate rose from 0.27% to 0.59%. Still very low in absolute terms, but worth monitoring; landing page relevance or ad–page alignment may be drifting.
  • Paid conversions slightly down (−1.9%) — despite the revenue increase, conversion volume fell marginally. The revenue gain is AOV-driven, not volume-driven.

Next Steps

  1. Instagram organic push: with only 16 sessions this week, prioritise at least 4–5 feed posts and 3 Reels for the coming week. Focus on lifestyle and product-in-home content aligned to the summer bedroom refresh season (July–August is approaching).
  2. TikTok activation: schedule at minimum 2 TikTok posts this week — room transformation or "what I ordered from Modish" format. Even low-frequency posting should outperform near-zero organic traffic. Test one video with trending HomeToK audio.
  3. Paid Social landing page audit: bounce rate doubled WoW on paid. Review the top-spend ad sets to confirm landing pages match creative messaging; prioritise ads pointing to bar stools and dining chairs (the brand's revenue bestsellers).
  4. Pinterest content calendar: sessions down −4.6% but this is a high-intent channel that drove 570 conversions. Increase pin frequency with seasonal bedroom and summer living content; board curation and fresh Idea Pins can sustain and grow this audience.
  5. Influencer brief for June: with organic traffic declining, initiate outreach to at least one UK home creator for a June collaboration — a post or reel featuring a Modish piece in a real styled home remains the most effective organic reach driver per the brand's ICP research.
