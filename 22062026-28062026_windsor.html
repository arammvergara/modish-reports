Highlights & Recommendations

✓ Wins This Week

  • Total social sessions up +11.3% WoW (6,868 vs 6,171) — strong top-of-funnel momentum heading into July.
  • Paid Social sessions grew +13.2% (6,324 vs 5,589) and conversions up +12.2% (9,346 vs 8,330). Volume is building.
  • Organic Social bounce rate improved: 1.10% vs 1.20% — engaged visitors from organic are sticking around more.
  • Organic Social revenue returned this week: £986 vs £0 last week — a meaningful signal that organic social is starting to convert.
  • User acquisition up across the board: +16.1% Paid Social users, +13.9% total social users WoW.

! Areas to Investigate

  • Paid Social revenue dropped sharply: £2,847 vs £5,182 (−45.1%) despite a +13.2% rise in sessions. Higher volume but lower revenue per conversion — check Meta AOV and product mix this week.
  • Organic Social sessions declining: 544 vs 582 (−6.5%) and conversions down −20.6% (1,036 vs 1,304). Organic content may need more consistency or stronger CTAs.
  • TikTok driving 0 measurable GA4 sessions — either content lacks link-through CTAs, or UTM parameters are missing. This is traffic that's invisible to the business.
  • Pinterest declining: organic sessions from Pinterest are within the Organic Social bucket (−6.5% overall). Native Pinterest impressions also down −23.6% WoW (see native social report).
  • Session duration not tracked: Windsor GA4 connector does not return avg_session_duration at channel group level — a gap worth monitoring through the GA4 dashboard directly.
Next Steps — Prioritised
  1. Investigate Paid Social revenue drop. Pull Meta Ads Manager for 22–28 Jun. Cross-reference GA4 purchase_revenue against Meta attributed revenue. Identify if AOV declined, product mix shifted, or if this is an attribution lag. Escalate to paid social agency if external.
  2. Fix TikTok attribution immediately. All TikTok bio links and content CTAs must use UTM-tagged URLs (utm_source=tiktok&utm_medium=social). Without this, TikTok-driven traffic is invisible in GA4 and cannot be measured or optimised.
  3. Audit organic social content this week. Review what posted 22–28 Jun across Instagram, Facebook, and Pinterest. Compare to 15–21 Jun (when conversions were higher at 1,304). Replicate whatever format or content type drove more conversions last week.
  4. Shift Pinterest strategy to beds & dining. Garden season is tapering. Begin pinning bedroom and autumn dining content for Jul–Aug aligned to the seasonal calendar. Schedule 3–5 new pins this week.
  5. Confirm July influencer activation. Monthly cadence requires at least 1 per month. If July's partnership isn't confirmed, brief and book now with a focus on bedroom styling. Ensure UTM links are in any bio or swipe-up placements.
