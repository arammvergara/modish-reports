Highlights & Recommendations

✓ What Worked

  • Instagram content views up +32.1% WoW (120,721 vs 91,406) and reach up +12.5% (33,538 vs 29,799) — strong top-of-funnel growth this week.
  • Instagram saves up +80% (36 vs 20) — a stronger save rate signals content people intend to act on later, a good leading indicator for purchase intent.
  • TikTok video views up +26.1% (4,169 vs 3,307) — reach is building even as deeper engagement metrics softened.
  • Facebook page visits and organic impressions both up (+4.6% and +10.5% respectively) — steady page-level discovery without paid support.
  • Facebook page fans essentially flat at 9,843 (+0.1%) — stable base heading into the beds selling season.

! Areas to Improve

  • Instagram engagement declining despite higher reach: total interactions down −16.6%, likes down −27.0%, comments down −76.9%. Review whether recent formats are driving views without prompting action — consider stronger CTAs in captions.
  • Pinterest impressions down −18.3% (84,593 vs 103,565) and outbound clicks down −21.8%. Garden season has peaked — shift boards and new pins toward bedroom and bed frame content for Jul–Aug.
  • TikTok engaged audience down −55.7% (35 vs 79) and likes down −47.8%, even as video views rose. This points to reach without resonance — test hook variations and stronger opening 3 seconds.
  • TikTok follower growth flat at 1,524 (0% change) for a second week. Growth needs a consistent posting cadence (3–5x per week) with trending audio.
  • Facebook post impressions (organic, post-level) down −34.7% (639 vs 978) even as page-level organic impressions rose — suggests recent individual posts underperformed relative to overall page reach.
