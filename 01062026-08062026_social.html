Highlights & actions
What worked
Instagram profile views up +9.6% to 127,755 — account discovery is strong even with lower reach, suggesting good SEO / hashtag visibility.
TikTok engagement up +56.3% and likes up +60% WoW — content resonance improved despite stable follower count and slightly fewer video views.
TikTok shares increased +50% (9 vs 6) — a positive signal that existing content is being shared more actively.
What to improve
Instagram reach fell -90% WoW (518 vs 5,159), returning to baseline after the previous week's influencer collaboration drove an above-average surge. This is an expected correction — not a posting gap — and the lower figure reflects our normal organic reach level without an active influencer partnership..
Pinterest impressions fell -38.2% and saves dropped -50% — the pinning cadence needs to increase; aim for at least 5–7 fresh pins this week.
Facebook engagement down -30.1% and page visits down -28.1% — review post frequency and creative format for the coming week.
Instagram website clicks still returning no data — confirm the link in bio is live and UTM-tracked.
